Our Services

UNIQUE VISITORS PER DAY - INCLUDING SPIDERS

Analyze the unique visitors to your website, including search engine crawlers (spiders).

OVERALL ANALYZED REQUESTS

Get insights into the total number of requests analyzed on your server.

REQUESTED FILES (URLS)

Monitor the specific files or URLs that are being requested on your website.

STATIC REQUESTS

Track the requests made for static files, such as images, CSS, and JavaScript.

NOT FOUND URLS

Identify URLs that return 404 (Not Found) errors.

VISITOR HOSTNAMES AND IPS

Analyze the hostnames and IP addresses of visitors accessing your website.

GEO LOCATION

Gain insights into the geographical locations of your website visitors.

HTTP STATUS CODES

Monitor the HTTP status codes returned by your server.

OPERATING SYSTEMS

Analyze the operating systems used by visitors accessing your website.

BROWSERS

Get insights into the web browsers used by visitors to access your website.
